Master those keyboard shortcuts
Some keyboard shortcuts for the most commonly used features in Lync.

  • Windows logo key+A: Accept an incoming invitation notification.
  • Windows log key+Esc: Decline an invitation notification.

    • In themain windowthe following shortcuts can be applied:

  • Ctrl+1: Go to the Contact list tab.
  • Ctrl+2: Go to the Activity Feeds tab.
  • Ctrl+3: Go to the Conversation list tab.
  • Ctrl+4: Go to the Phone tab.
  • Alt+A: View all conversations when in the Conversation list tab.
  • Alt+D: View missed conversations when in Conversation list tab.
  • Alt+C: View missed calls when in Conversation list tab.

Changing your status
Being an instant messaging platform, showing your status and availability to other users (fellow colleagues is very important)
Under your profile, located at the top of the main window with your name and profile picture, is a place to set your status.
Click on the drop down arrow next to your current status, to show the other status options available.
Click a status, to set your current status.

Adding people to a conversation
Adding colleagues to a conference call: Drag a contact from theLync main windowand drop it into theConversation windowto add the person to the conference call.

The lync lobby
The virtual lobby in Lync is used when you setup conference calls. This lobby allows people who call into the meeting to know that they are in the right spot, and you (or the creator of the conversation) to act as the "receptionist" to decide who can get into the call, and who can not.
Once you have set up a new meeting, navigate to Meeting Options on the Home tab, and select the meeting options you want.
Once people dial in, you will be notified that there are people in the lobby waiting, and will be able to control the attendance.

Finding experts
Stumped on a subject matter? Needed help with something specific. Lync will help you find experts who have matching skills in their SharePoint profile, making it easy to make contact when in need of assistance.

Activity update feed
"Out to a client site visit" Use Lync to let your follow colleagues know where you are. The Activity update feed on Lync allows everyone to stay informed about important milestones; such as news and organisation changed.
The activity update feed is accessed by the button menu next to your contacts, and clicking on it will allow you to check the latest status of your contacts.
